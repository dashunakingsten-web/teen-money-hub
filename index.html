3 rules I follow so I don't get scammed

  1. Never pay to "unlock" a job or survey site.
    Real money apps are always free. If they ask for payment upfront, RUN.
  2. Use a separate email for money apps.
    Keep it away from your personal email to avoid hacks affecting your main account.
  3. If it sounds like magic money, it's probably fake.
    "Make $10k/week from home!" = SCAM. Real apps pay small amounts for real work.
